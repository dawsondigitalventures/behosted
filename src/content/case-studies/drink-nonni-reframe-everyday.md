Some drinks are built for function. Nonni had the opportunity to become something more.

In a saturated electrolyte market dominated by performance messaging and wellness clichés, Nonni already stood apart through its flavour and branding. But there was an opportunity to push it further. To position it not just as a drink you need, but one you want to be seen with.

## Working with us

Our role at Behosted was to develop a content direction that felt culturally relevant, visually distinctive and social-first.

From the outset, the focus was clear. Elevate Nonni beyond traditional product photography and into a lifestyle space that felt current and aspirational. We explored how the brand could exist naturally within moments its audience already engages with such as fashion, interiors and everyday rituals, without feeling staged or overly commercial.

This became our starting point.

We built a visual world where Nonni could sit comfortably alongside objects typically associated with style and self-expression. Statement heels, vintage magazines, citrus textures and curated fridge interiors all played a role. The intention was to create imagery that felt effortless but considered. The kind of content that stops the scroll because it does not immediately read as an ad.

## The outcome

The concept centred around elevated everyday placement.

Rather than isolating the product, we integrated Nonni into unexpected but relatable settings. A fridge became a canvas, stocked not just with drinks but with personality. Bold red heels sat alongside chilled cans. A copy of Vogue introduced a fashion context. Fresh citrus added vibrancy and tied back to flavour cues.

Each composition was designed to feel slightly unconventional, balancing familiarity with surprise. The styling leaned into a clean but playful aesthetic, allowing the product to remain central while still feeling part of a wider lifestyle.

The still imagery was captured with social platforms in mind, ensuring versatility across Instagram and other channels. Each shot was designed to feel native. Something you might save, share or come back to, rather than simply scroll past.

## The end result

The final content repositioned Nonni within its category.

Instead of competing on functionality alone, the brand stepped into a more cultural space, one that aligns with taste, style and identity. The imagery presents Nonni as the cool go-to. A drink that belongs just as much in a curated lifestyle moment as it does in a gym bag.

That shift is subtle but important. It moves the brand from product to presence.

At Behosted, we work with brands to create content that does not just showcase what they are, but redefines how they are perceived. With Nonni, that meant transforming everyday placements into something aspirational, and making electrolyte drinks feel like part of the aesthetic conversation.
