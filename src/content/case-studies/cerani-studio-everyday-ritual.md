Some objects quietly shape the rhythm of our days. The cup we reach for first thing in the morning. The plate that sits at the centre of a table when friends arrive. Cerani Studio creates ceramics that are made for those moments, pieces that feel sculptural but never untouchable.

When we began working with Cerani Studio, the aim was not to create a traditional campaign or a polished product shoot. Instead, we wanted to understand the brand at a deeper level. What they wanted to be known for. How their pieces were meant to feel in the hands. Which elements of their identity they wanted to lean into visually.

From those early conversations, The Everyday Ritual was born. A concept rooted in calm, tactility and the beauty of everyday moments, designed to reflect Cerani Studio's approach to form and function.

## Working with us

At Behosted, every shoot begins with collaboration. We work closely with our clients to define the story they want to tell, then translate that into a considered visual direction. For Cerani Studio, this meant creating a detailed shoot moodboard that explored supporting pieces, backgrounds, environments and light. Neutral tones, soft morning light and layered textures formed the foundation, allowing the ceramics to sit naturally within the space. Once the direction was signed off, the focus shifted to bringing that concept to life.

## The outcome

The story unfolded across three moments, each reflecting a different rhythm of the day.

Morning began slowly, with breakfast in bed. This scene was about romanticising the everyday and leaning into softness. Luxury pastries were styled on Cerani Studio plates, fresh coffee poured into their minimal cups, and a yoghurt bowl filled with seasonal fruits, figs, fresh seeds and golden honey. The atmosphere was calm and indulgent, but never out of reach, capturing the quiet luxury of an unhurried morning.

![Breakfast in bed with Cerani Studio ceramics](/images/case-studies/Cerani-Studio/Cerani_Studio_bedside_shot.jpg)

As the day progressed, the setting moved into lunch with friends. A neutral table was styled with Cerani Studio plates, serving pieces, candles and candlestick holders. The food was simple and designed for sharing, with fresh bread, cheese and crackers creating a relaxed, social feel. This moment focused on connection and ease, showing how Cerani pieces elevate a gathering without feeling formal or overstyled.

![A light lunch spread for friends dining](/images/case-studies/Cerani-Studio/Cerani_Studio_table_setting.jpg)

The final scene was dinner, stripped back and understated. A fresh vegetable soup with a side of soft dough was styled against a simple background, allowing the texture and form of the ceramics to take centre stage. It was a quiet end to the day, centred on comfort and simplicity.

![Fresh vegetable soup and focaccia dinner](/images/case-studies/Cerani-Studio/Cerani_Studio_place_set.jpg)

## The end result

Once the shoot was complete, the final imagery was curated and shared back with Cerani Studio, ready to be used across their website, social channels and wider brand touchpoints. Content designed not just to look beautiful, but to tell a cohesive story.

This is where Behosted sits best. We help brands bring concepts to life through thoughtful content creation, crafting visuals that feel intentional, human and grounded in real moments. Working with Cerani Studio was an exercise in trust, collaboration and storytelling, resulting in imagery that feels lived in, honest and true to the brand.
